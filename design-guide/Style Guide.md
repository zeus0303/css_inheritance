# blog article style guide

## Colors

- Title & body: #414141
- Subtitle: #7D7D7D
- Links: #CA5E10;

---

## Typography

- Title: Cinzel 900
- Subtitle & body: PT Serif 400 | line-height: 1.6

### Font sizes

- title: 96px                     6rem
- Subtitle: 36px                      2.25
- Lead paragraph: 21px       1.3125
- Body: 18px                            1.125

#### Pro tip
- Use a more steep line-height for larger text
- Ideal line height is between 1.2 - 1.6 for smaller text
